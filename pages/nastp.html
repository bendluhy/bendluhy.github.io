About the Program

Through the North Attleboro Senior Technology Program (NASTP), I visit senior centers across the community to help older adults understand and use modern devices. I assist with everything from smartphones and computers to email, settings, apps, and general troubleshooting. My goal is to make technology less intimidating and help seniors stay connected with family and friends.

What I Do

  • Visit multiple senior centers on a regular basis to offer in-person support.
  • Help seniors learn to use smartphones, tablets, and computers.
  • Teach essential digital skills such as email, messaging, video calls, and device settings.
  • Assist with everyday troubleshooting and technical questions.
  • Provide patient, one-on-one guidance in a friendly and supportive environment.
